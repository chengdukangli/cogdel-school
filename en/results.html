Admissions Counselling Centre

A dedicated university admissions and career development centre, led by a University of Nottingham (UK) Master of Education, with British Council-certified university admissions counsellors and 10 years of international education experience.

The centre maintains close cooperation with admissions officers from multiple countries, developing precise admissions strategies for every student. The full process is covered: subject selection advice, competition planning, personal statement writing, and interview coaching.

Cogdel's Philosophy

"The happiest career choice lies at the intersection of what you love, what you are good at, and what the world needs." University planning is a "through-train" programme from Grade 6 to graduation -- not a last-minute push in the final year.
