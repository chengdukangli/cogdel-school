Admissions: Cogdel Students Shine Across the United Kingdom

February 9, 2026 | Cogdel School Chengdu

Cogdel School Chengdu's graduating class has received an impressive and geographically diverse range of offers from leading UK universities spanning England, Scotland, and Wales.

UK Offer Highlights

England: Offers from Russell Group institutions including Manchester, Bristol, Leeds, Sheffield, Birmingham, Nottingham, Southampton, and Liverpool — plus specialist institutions in art, design, and music.

Scotland: University of Edinburgh and University of Glasgow — both members of the prestigious Russell Group, with Edinburgh consistently ranked among the UK's top 10.

Wales: Cardiff University — a Russell Group member and one of Britain's major civic universities.

What This Breadth Tells Us

The geographic and institutional diversity of these offers reflects Cogdel's philosophy: we don't push all students toward the same shortlist of "brand name" universities. We help each student find their best fit — academically, culturally, and personally. The UK higher education landscape is rich and varied, and Cogdel students are accessing its full breadth.

Beyond Rankings

"The UK has over 130 universities, many world-class in specific fields. Our job is to match each student with institutions where they will thrive — not just survive. Sometimes that's Cambridge; sometimes it's a specialist program at a university that doesn't top the global rankings but leads its field. Both choices are valid, and both represent success." — Liz Li, Head of University Guidance
