Cogdel Students Excel in Intermediate Biology Olympiad / IBO 2025

December 9, 2025 | Cogdel School Chengdu

Cogdel School Chengdu students have achieved strong results in the 2025 UK Biology Olympiad (Intermediate) / IBO qualifying round, with multiple awards at Gold, Silver, and Bronze levels and several students scoring in the top global percentiles.

The IBO Pathway

The Intermediate Biology Olympiad serves as a critical stepping stone toward the full International Biology Olympiad — one of the world's most prestigious science competitions. Success at this level demonstrates a deep understanding of biological principles across molecular biology, genetics, ecology, and physiology, as well as the ability to apply that understanding to novel experimental scenarios.

Cogdel's Life Sciences Strength

Biology is a particular strength at Cogdel, supported by our 40% laboratory experiment ratio and faculty who bring genuine research experience to their teaching. Students don't just learn about experimental design — they design and conduct experiments. By the time they sit for competitions like the BBO/IBO, they've internalized scientific thinking as a habit, not a test strategy.

From the Biology Department

"Biology competitions reward a specific kind of thinking: the ability to look at unfamiliar data, identify patterns, generate hypotheses, and evaluate evidence. These are exactly the skills our students practice every week in the lab. The medals are recognition of genuine scientific capability, not exam-cramming."

Congratulations to all participants and to the Biology faculty for their dedicated mentorship.
