Cogdel 2026-2027 Admissions Brochure

June 1, 2026 | Cogdel School Chengdu

Cogdel School Chengdu has officially released the 2026-2027 Admissions Brochure, providing families with a comprehensive overview of our K-12 international education programs. The brochure is designed to answer every question a prospective family might have — from curriculum pathways to boarding life, from faculty credentials to university destinations.

School Overview

Cogdel School Chengdu is a K-12 fully boarding international school situated within the National 5A-rated scenic area of Anren Ancient Town. The school sits among 40 historical Republic-era protected buildings with 52% green coverage. Squirrels and peacocks roam freely across a campus that balances century-old heritage with state-of-the-art facilities.

At a Glance

80+ years of historical heritage | 1:3 student-teacher ratio | 97% World TOP100 admission rate | 60% Master's/PhD faculty | 100% faculty with overseas experience | 21 A-Level subject choices | 100% art-track students to QS Art TOP10 for 5 consecutive years

Our Accreditations

Cogdel holds multiple internationally recognized accreditations:

  • Cambridge Assessment International Education (CAIE) — official IGCSE and A-Level examination centre
  • Pearson Edexcel — official IGCSE and A-Level examination centre
  • Cambridge English Assessment — official KET/PET examination centre
  • US College Board — AP course authorized
  • IB-PYP (Primary Years Programme) — official candidate school
  • Cognia — global school accreditation
  • Duke of Edinburgh's International Award — execution centre
  • Ministry of Education — recognized "Sino-Foreign Cultural Exchange Characteristic School"

Curriculum Pathways

Primary (Grades 1-5): IB-PYP framework integrated with the Chinese National Compulsory Education curriculum. Chinese and Math aligned to top-tier public school standards in central Chengdu. English delivered through cross-grade leveled teaching with Chinese-foreign co-teacher teams in an immersive bilingual environment. Target: KET highest level by Grade 5. Second foreign languages in French and Japanese are also offered.

Middle School (Grades 6-8): National curriculum blended with international school-based content (integrating UK Key Stage 3). Grade 6 begins middle school to allow early secondary science exposure. Lab experiment ratio raised to 40%. English taught via CLIL methodology; target: PET level by Grade 8. Career exploration starts from Grade 6. 100% student participation in competitions including AMC, BPhO, UKChO, and NEC.

High School (Grades 9-12): IGCSE + A-Level program with 21 subject options and free course selection across arts, sciences, and creative disciplines. A 1+N Mentor System pairs each student with a class mentor, academic mentor, competition mentor, and university guidance mentor. AP courses available; IBDP under application.

Faculty Excellence

40% of faculty graduated from UK G5 super-elite universities and US Ivy League institutions. 60% hold Master's or PhD degrees. 100% have overseas study or work experience. 100% hold official A-Level/AP teaching certifications. Faculty come from institutions including LSE, UCL, Imperial College London, University of Pennsylvania, Tsinghua, Peking, and Zhejiang universities.

University Results

97% of Cogdel graduates enter World TOP100 universities. Art students have achieved a 100% placement rate to QS Art & Design TOP10 institutions for five consecutive years, with art exam A/B rates exceeding 90%. Students have received offers from Cambridge, Oxford, LSE, Imperial College, UCL, University of Chicago, Carnegie Mellon, USC, University of Toronto, UBC, and many more.
