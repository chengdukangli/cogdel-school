Admissions Process

Step 1: Consultation -- Discuss your child's grade, learning background, interests, and family priorities with our admissions team.

Step 2: Campus Visit -- Experience our campus environment, curriculum highlights, student support systems, and boarding life firsthand.

Step 3: Assessment -- A comprehensive evaluation including academic testing, English assessment, student interview, and portfolio review (for art applicants).

Step 4: Admissions Discussion -- Based on assessment results, we provide curriculum pathway recommendations, university direction guidance, and admissions communication.

Step 5: Enrolment -- Complete the enrolment confirmation, submit registration and entry documents, and prepare for pre-term bridging.
