School Overview

Cogdel School Chengdu is a K-12 fully boarding international school located in the ancient town of Anren in Dayi County, Chengdu. The school sits within a nationally rated 5A scenic area, surrounded by 80 years of heritage Republican-era architecture.

The campus is home to 40 historic heritage buildings, with 52% green coverage where squirrels and peacocks roam freely. Education unfolds naturally in an environment where historic charm meets modern facilities -- carrying the weight of history while embracing the energy of the future.

International Accreditations

Cogdel School Chengdu holds multiple internationally recognized accreditations:

  • Cambridge Assessment International Education (CAIE) -- Official IGCSE/A-Level Examination Centre
  • Pearson Edexcel -- Official IGCSE/A-Level Examination Centre
  • Cambridge English Assessment -- Official KET/PET Examination Centre
  • College Board (USA) -- Authorized AP Course Provider

Principal's Message

Amy Lu (Lu Kun)

Education: B.Eng. in Aerospace Engineering, Tsinghua University; MBA, China Europe International Business School (CEIBS)

Career: Former aircraft design engineer at AVIC; former Investment Director

Principal Lu designed Cogdel around three education pain points:

A Future-Ready School -- Breaking the single assessment system. Cultivating irreplaceable abilities for the AI era. Replacing traditional report cards with the "8-Dimension Competency Portfolio."

A People-First School -- Connecting school with society. Through project-based learning, career exploration, the Investors' Classroom, and Career Fairs, students engage with how the real world operates while still in school.

A School That Cultivates Leaders -- Making soft skills development scientific rather than mystical. Tracking leadership growth by semester, recording every step of progress with data.

Three Education Pain Points

We Start from the Problems

Pain Point 1: Breaking the Single Assessment System

Traditional schools use written exam scores as the sole standard, which inherently undermines the confidence of at least 80% of students. Cogdel replaces traditional report cards with the "8-Dimension Competency Portfolio" -- every teacher becomes a "sensor" that discovers growth.

Pain Point 2: Connecting School with Society

Through project-based learning, career exploration starting in Grade 6, the Investors' Classroom, and Career Fairs, we broaden students' horizons and prevent them from focusing solely on "how to pass exams."

Pain Point 3: Making Soft Skills Development Scientific

Using engineering logic, we transform soft skills development from a black box into something trackable, assessable, and manageable -- turning it into a science.

8-Dimension Competency

Cogdel's proprietary "8-Dimension Competency Portfolio" covers eight core dimensions of student development:

Global Vision

Global perspective and cross-cultural understanding

Learning Ability

Self-directed learning, critical thinking, and knowledge transfer

Physical & Mental Health

Physical strength and psychological resilience

Intrinsic Motivation

Genuine passion for learning and desire for growth

Innovation

Courage to experiment and talent for creation

Leadership

Making decisions and leading teams to solve problems in complex situations

Collaboration

Listening, communicating, compromising, and leading

Responsibility

Caring about social issues and being willing to take on responsibility

In a Nutshell

Using engineering logic to design future-ready education. At Cogdel, every child's growth is seen, recorded, and cherished.
