Three Gold, Two Silver, Two Bronze — Cogdel Students Achieve 40%+ Award Rate

May 7, 2026 | Cogdel School Chengdu

Cogdel School Chengdu students have delivered an outstanding performance in the latest round of international academic competitions, bringing home 3 Gold, 2 Silver, and 2 Bronze medals with an overall award rate exceeding 40% — significantly above the global average.

The Results in Detail

  • 3 Gold Medals — Top 10% of competitors globally. These students demonstrated mastery of advanced content and exceptional problem-solving ability under timed conditions.
  • 2 Silver Medals — Top 25% globally. Strong performances reflecting deep understanding and effective test strategy.
  • 2 Bronze Medals — Top 40% globally. Solid achievements that place these students well above the international average.

The Bigger Picture

These results span multiple subject areas — a testament to the breadth of Cogdel's academic strength rather than excellence in a single niche. More importantly, every medal represents months of disciplined preparation: students learning to manage their time between regular coursework and competition training, developing resilience through practice setbacks, and performing at their peak under pressure.

From the Competition Coordinator

"We don't train students to win medals — we use competitions as a vehicle for developing the habits of excellence: goal-setting, sustained effort, learning from failure, performing under pressure. The medals are a byproduct. The growth is the point."

How Cogdel Supports Competition Success

100% of Cogdel students participate in academic competitions — it's not an optional extra for a gifted few. Our competition program includes: dedicated preparation sessions integrated into the academic timetable, subject-specialist faculty who understand competition formats, peer study groups for collaborative problem-solving, and a culture that celebrates effort and improvement as much as results.

Congratulations to all seven award recipients and to the faculty mentors who guided their preparation. Your hard work and intellectual courage inspire the entire Cogdel community.
