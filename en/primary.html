Primary School (Grades 1-5)

IB-PYP Integration | Dual-Track Development | Personalized Customization

Four Core Features

Dual-Track Development + IB-PYP Inquiry + Differentiated English + Personalized Customization

1. Dual-Track Development

Chinese and Mathematics are benchmarked against top-tier public schools in central Chengdu, while integrating international teaching methods. This supports diverse future pathways without sacrificing academic foundations.

This means children build a solid foundation in China's foundational education while gaining the vision and methodology of international education -- both paths remain open.

2. IB-PYP Inquiry

Through six transdisciplinary theme inquiry units, students develop critical thinking, creative thinking, communication skills, and cross-disciplinary research ability:

  • Who We Are -- Exploring identity, beliefs, and values
  • Where We Are in Place and Time -- Understanding history, geography, and culture
  • How We Express Ourselves -- Developing creativity and communication
  • How the World Works -- Exploring scientific principles and natural laws
  • How We Organize Ourselves -- Understanding social systems and organizations
  • Sharing the Planet -- Focusing on environment, sustainability, and social responsibility

3. Differentiated English Instruction

Cross-grade differentiated instruction with foreign and Chinese teachers collaborating on lesson planning and delivery, creating an immersive bilingual environment:

  • Students are grouped by English proficiency level, not by grade
  • Foreign and Chinese teachers collaborate to ensure teaching quality
  • KET highest level achieved by Grade 5
  • Additional second languages offered: French, Japanese, and more

4. Personalized Customization

"One Student, One Group" Personalized Feedback

Weekly -- Academic summaries with timely learning feedback

Monthly -- PYP inquiry reports tracking growth trajectories

Each Semester -- Three-way conference (student-parent-teacher) for full-cycle home-school collaboration

Real Case: One Student, One Timetable

A Grade 5 student whose English is at primary level but whose physics, chemistry, and biology have reached secondary level. The school designed a cross-division personalized timetable -- English and humanities classes in the primary division, science classes crossing into the middle school division for lab experiments and research. This requires coordinating timetables, classrooms, and teachers across divisions, but the school is willing to make personalized matches for every child's needs.

Enrichment Programmes

Dozens of enrichment courses for students to choose from:

  • Junior Chef -- Life skills and creativity
  • Woodworking -- Hands-on practice and spatial thinking
  • Dance -- Artistic expression and physical coordination
  • Ceramics -- Traditional craftsmanship and aesthetic development
  • Piano -- Musical literacy and artistic cultivation
  • Rugby -- Teamwork and sportsmanship
  • Maths Excellence -- Subject enrichment
  • AMC Gifted Camp -- Competition preparation
  • English Dubbing -- Language expression and arts
  • AI General Studies -- Future-ready digital literacy
