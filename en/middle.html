Middle School (Grades 6-8)

China-West Fusion | 40% Science Lab Ratio | Full Competition Coverage | Early Career Planning

Smooth Primary-to-Secondary Transition

Middle school is the stage when students are most likely to feel lost. Cogdel has designed a systematic "smooth primary-to-secondary transition." Grade 6 students join the Middle School division early, starting secondary science courses while continuing IB-PYP inquiry, fully considering adaptation needs during the transition period.

China-West Fusion Curriculum

Mathematics and Sciences based on the national curriculum, integrated with UK Key Stage 3:

  • National Curriculum as Foundation -- Ensuring completeness and depth of the knowledge system
  • UK KS3 Integration -- Introducing international teaching methods and assessment systems
  • 40% Science Lab Ratio -- Emphasizing hands-on inquiry rather than rote textbook learning
  • 2+ Cross-Disciplinary Research Projects Per Year -- Annual research fair with public presentation and assessment

Early Career Planning

University planning starts in Grade 6 with a dedicated admissions mentor:

  • Subject Exploration -- Exposing students to different academic disciplines to discover interests
  • Personal Assessment -- Understanding students' academic strengths and developmental potential
  • Career Exploration -- Through real company visits and STEM institution placements, understanding societal needs

English Intensification

Layered instruction following the European CLIL (Content and Language Integrated Learning) approach:

  • CLIL Methodology -- Learning science, history and other subjects through English
  • Academic English Focus -- Not just daily conversation, but academic expression competence
  • PET Achieved by Grade 8 -- Building a solid language foundation for the IGCSE stage in high school

Full Competition Coverage

100% Student Participation in International Competitions

The goal is to find "a sense of achievement and mastery," not to create an arms race. Competitions include BPhO (British Physics Olympiad), AMC10 (American Mathematics Competition), UKChO (UK Chemistry Olympiad), NEC (National Economics Challenge), and more. On-campus mentors provide full guidance throughout.
