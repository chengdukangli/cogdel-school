Our Approach

We believe that real growth is not measured solely by exams, but by each child's progress across eight dimensions of competency. Every child is seen, valued, and nurtured.

8-Dimension Competency Portfolio

Cogdel's proprietary "8-Dimension Competency Portfolio" covers eight core areas, with teachers acting as "sensors" that discover and record growth:

  • Vision -- Global perspective and cross-cultural understanding
  • Learning Ability -- Self-directed learning, critical thinking, knowledge transfer
  • Physical & Mental Health -- Physical strength and psychological resilience
  • Intrinsic Motivation -- Genuine passion for learning and desire for growth
  • Innovation -- Courage to experiment and talent for creation
  • Leadership -- Making decisions and leading teams in complex situations
  • Collaboration -- Listening, communicating, compromising, and leading
  • Responsibility -- Caring about social issues and taking on responsibility

Sports Education

"Sports Are the Primary Source of Confidence"

Principal Lu firmly believes that "sports are the primary source of confidence," because sports give children the most direct and honest feedback: running faster, jumping higher, persisting longer -- no "grading" required, the body tells the answer.

Sports teach three things:

  • Losing Gracefully -- Winning and losing on the sports field is normal; more valuable than "always winning"
  • Persistence -- Pushing one more breath at the limit internalizes into lifelong resilience
  • Teamwork -- Basketball, football, relay races -- learning "how to succeed with others"

The school has built a multi-sport field and offers parkour, rugby, badminton, volleyball, tennis and more.

Device Management Policy

"Self-Discipline and Freedom Are Two Sides of the Same Coin"

Cogdel's scientific device management system was collaboratively designed by students, parents, and the school:

  • Boarding students hand in phones to life mentors upon return each Sunday
  • No student is allowed to carry phones during daytime on weekdays
  • 30 minutes of phone use every other evening to contact family
  • Course-specific devices available by teacher request

Student Self-Governance

The Student Council Is Not a "Little Officials' Club"

At Cogdel, the student council is a genuine self-governance organization. Candidates must deliver speeches, face questions, and win votes from the entire student body. The process itself is the most vivid lesson in democracy and leadership.
