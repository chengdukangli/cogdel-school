Cogdel Students Excel in 2026 American Mathematics Competition

March 12, 2026 | Cogdel School Chengdu

Cogdel School Chengdu students delivered exceptional results in the 2026 American Mathematics Competition (AMC), with multiple students qualifying for the American Invitational Mathematics Examination (AIME) — a distinction reserved for approximately the top 2.5% of AMC participants worldwide.

About the AMC

The AMC is the most widely recognized mathematics competition for secondary school students, administered by the Mathematical Association of America (MAA). Success in the AMC opens a progressive pathway: AMC → AIME → USA Mathematical Olympiad (USAMO) → International Mathematical Olympiad (IMO). For university applications — particularly to STEM programs at top US and UK universities — strong AMC/AIME results are a significant differentiator.

Cogdel's Results

  • Multiple AIME qualifiers across both AMC 10 (Grades 9-10) and AMC 12 (Grades 11-12) categories
  • Several students scored in the top 1% globally
  • Strong performances from both junior and senior cohorts — indicating deep mathematical culture across grade levels, not just isolated talent in a single year group

From the Mathematics Department

"Mathematics at Cogdel is not about drilling formulas or practicing past papers endlessly. Our students learn to approach unfamiliar problems creatively — to sit with a problem they don't immediately know how to solve and work through it systematically. That's the difference between exam preparation and mathematical thinking. AMC rewards the latter."

Supporting Mathematical Excellence

Cogdel's mathematics program includes: the Excellence Math extension track for students ready for additional challenge, the AMC Genius Camp for competition-specific preparation, and interdisciplinary opportunities to apply mathematical thinking in science, economics (NEC competition), and project-based research. Mathematics is not taught as an isolated skill — it's integrated into the broader intellectual culture of the school.

Congratulations to all AMC participants and AIME qualifiers. Whether you scored in the top 1% or took on the challenge for the first time, the courage to compete is itself a victory.
