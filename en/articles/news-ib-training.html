New Year, New Journey! Cogdel Primary Faculty Complete Official IB-PYP Training

March 9, 2026 | Cogdel School Chengdu

Cogdel School Chengdu kicked off 2026 with a significant milestone: all Primary division faculty completed official IB-PYP (International Baccalaureate Primary Years Programme) training, delivered by IB-certified workshop leaders. This comprehensive training marks a major step in Cogdel's journey toward full IB-PYP authorization.

The Training Program

The intensive multi-day training covered the full spectrum of PYP pedagogy:

  • Inquiry-Based Learning: Designing units of inquiry that start with student questions, not teacher answers. Teachers learned to structure provocations that spark genuine curiosity and to facilitate rather than direct the learning process.
  • Transdisciplinary Themes: Connecting knowledge across traditional subject boundaries through six organizing themes — Who We Are, Where We Are in Place and Time, How We Express Ourselves, How the World Works, How We Organize Ourselves, and Sharing the Planet.
  • Concept-Driven Curriculum: Moving beyond facts to transferable big ideas. Rather than teaching "the water cycle," teachers learned to teach through the conceptual lens of "systems" — making learning applicable across contexts.
  • Assessment in the PYP: Gathering evidence of learning through observation, performance tasks, portfolios, and student reflection — not just tests.
  • The IB Learner Profile: Cultivating attributes including inquiry, knowledge, thinking, communication, principled action, open-mindedness, caring, risk-taking, balance, and reflection.

Why IB-PYP Matters

The IB Primary Years Programme is widely recognized as the gold standard for primary education worldwide. Unlike traditional curricula that treat subjects as isolated silos, PYP frames learning as an integrated, inquiry-driven process that develops the whole child — intellectually, socially, emotionally, and physically.

From the PYP Coordinator

"The PYP philosophy aligns beautifully with Cogdel's 8-Dimension Competency Framework. Both ask the same fundamental question: what kind of person do we want our students to become — not just what do we want them to know? The synthesis of IB inquiry methodology with Cogdel's engineering approach to competency development creates something genuinely distinctive."

What This Means for Cogdel Families

For current and prospective Cogdel Primary families, IB-PYP authorization means their children experience a curriculum designed to develop learners who ask great questions, think across boundaries, and take meaningful action — not passive recipients of information. Combined with Cogdel's Chinese/Math alignment to top-tier public school standards, students get the best of both worlds: rigorous foundations in core subjects and globally recognized inquiry pedagogy.

Cogdel Primary is on track for full IB-PYP authorization. Follow "Cogdel School Chengdu" on WeChat for updates on this exciting journey.
