Cogdel Leadership Team

Chinese and International Senior Educators | 15+ Years International Education Experience | Led by a Tsinghua + Investor Background Principal

Core Philosophy of the Leadership Team

Cogdel's leadership team is composed of experienced Chinese and international educators, led by a principal with Tsinghua University and investment industry background. They infuse engineering logic and investment thinking into educational management, committed to "cultivating leaders who walk the world with pride."

Team Characteristics

Led by the Principal -- Principal Lu personally participates in academic management. Professional Specialization -- Separate dedicated leaders for academics, student development, and university admissions. Chinese-Western Integration -- Both educators deeply versed in China's foundational education and international school operations experts. Small and Selective -- Approximately 100 students enrolled each year, ensuring attention to every child.

Amy Lu (Lu Kun)

Chair of the Board & Executive Principal

Education: B.Eng. in Aerospace Engineering, Tsinghua University; MBA, China Europe International Business School (CEIBS)

Career: Former aircraft design engineer at AVIC; former Investment Director

Principal Lu is the driving force behind Cogdel. She designed this school around three education pain points: breaking the single assessment system, connecting school with society, and making soft skills development scientific rather than mystical.

As a Tsinghua alumna and former technology investor, she infuses engineering logic and investment thinking into educational management, personally teaching the Investors' Classroom and AI General Studies courses.
