Free Course Selection

The high school is built around the philosophy of "free course selection," with flexible combinations across humanities, sciences, and arts to match university admissions pathways in different countries and regions.

The "1+N Mentor System"

Customized Development Plans for Every Student

1 Homeroom Mentor -- Comprehensive oversight of daily learning and life

+ Academic Mentor -- Targeted tutoring for academic weak points

+ Competition Mentor -- Professional guidance for international competitions

+ Admissions Mentor -- Full process: subject selection, competition planning, personal statement writing, interview coaching

US University Dual-Enrolment Courses

The specially designed URP / Design Thinking dual-enrolment course allows students to earn overseas university credits while still in high school, adding a competitive edge to US university applications.

IELTS Specialist Custom Courses

Specially recruited IELTS expert teachers deliver customized language courses, allowing students to complete standardized English test preparation on campus without needing external training.

Art Pathway: Global Art School Programme

100% QS TOP10 for 5 Consecutive Years

Open to Grade 9 students, offering a customized development pathway for art-focused students.

Curriculum: "2 years IGCSE + 2 years A-Level" structure. Covering five pathways: Fine Art, Architecture, Animation, Illustration, and Music. Supported by dedicated studios, photography studio, digital media creation lab, and more.

Results: Art exam A/B rate exceeds 90% (far above global average); 100% of graduates in the past 5 years have received offers from QS Art & Design TOP10 institutions; top A-Level Art & Design scores in China for two consecutive years. Universities include University of the Arts London (UAL), Parsons School of Design, Berklee College of Music, ArtCenter College of Design, and more.

Academic Support

  • Subject Tutoring -- Targeted support for weaker subjects
  • New Student Bridging Programme -- Helping transfer students make a smooth transition
  • Exam Preparation Plans -- Scientific test preparation strategies
  • Competition Coaching -- Full guidance from on-campus mentors

Graduates are admitted to top universities in the UK, US, Canada, Australia, Singapore, and Hong Kong SAR.
